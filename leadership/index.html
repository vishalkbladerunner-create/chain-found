How We Lead

Operators, Allocators, and Builders

Operate

A 24/7 execution team made up of crypto operators who have shipped Top-80 protocols, run launches, and managed treasuries through every market cycle since 2017.

Build

In-house engineering, product, and design partner with founders inside a 12-week sprint — tokenomics, smart contracts, custody architecture, and product all built in parallel rather than in sequence.
